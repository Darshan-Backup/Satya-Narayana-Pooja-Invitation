About Anant Chaturdashi - Double Divine Blessings

Anant Chaturdashi holds special significance as it marks the culmination of Ganesh Chaturthi while simultaneously being an auspicious day for Satya Narayana Pooja. This divine confluence brings together the blessings of both Lord Ganesha and Lord Vishnu (Satya Narayana), creating a powerful spiritual atmosphere for devotees.

Lord Ganesha's Grand Farewell

Anant Chaturdashi marks the final day of the 11-day Ganesh Chaturthi festival. On this day, devotees bid farewell to Lord Ganesha with great devotion and grandeur, believing that Ganesha takes away all obstacles and negative energies while blessing them with wisdom and prosperity.

Satya Narayana's Divine Protection

Performing Satya Narayana Pooja on Anant Chaturdashi invokes the infinite (Anant) blessings of Lord Vishnu. This sacred ritual brings divine protection, prosperity, and fulfillment of righteous desires to all participants and their families.

Why This Dual Celebration Is Special

The combination of Ganesha Visarjan and Satya Narayana Pooja on Anant Chaturdashi creates a unique spiritual opportunity. As we bid farewell to Lord Ganesha (who removes obstacles), we simultaneously invoke Lord Satya Narayana (who provides divine protection and blessings). This ensures continuous divine grace and protection for the year ahead.
